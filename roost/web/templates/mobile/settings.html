Getting Started with Roost

Your personal productivity hub — tasks, calendar, email, and more in one place.

Quick Start

Roost gives you a unified view of your tasks, calendar, and email. Access it via:

  • Web: This app — bookmark it or add to your home screen
  • Telegram: Send /start to the bot for interactive commands
Tasks & Projects

Create and manage tasks with priorities, deadlines, and project grouping.

  • Web: Tap Tasks in the bottom bar, then + to add
  • Telegram: /add Buy groceries — quick-add a task
  • Telegram: /tasks — browse your task list
  • Telegram: /done 42 — mark task #42 complete
Calendar

View your calendar events and task deadlines in one place.

  • Web: Tap Calendar in the bottom bar
  • Telegram: /cal — today's events
  • Telegram: /block 14:00 Team standup — create a calendar event

Calendar source depends on your instance: Google Calendar or Microsoft 365.

Email Triage

Triage your inbox without leaving Telegram. View, reply, archive, and create tasks from emails.

  • /inbox — browse your latest unread emails
  • Tap View to read a thread, AI Draft for a suggested reply
  • Tap Archive to clean up, Task to capture an action item
Presentations

Generate slide decks and structure meeting notes using AI.

  • /deck Company overview for investors — generates a .pptx
  • /mnotes [paste raw notes] — structures meeting notes
Voice Notes

Send voice messages to the Telegram bot. They're transcribed and routed by prefix:

  • "task: ..." — creates a task from your voice
  • "note: ..." — saves a note
  • "deck: ..." — generates a presentation
  • "meeting: ..." — structures as meeting notes
  • No prefix — saved as a general journal entry
Daily Briefing

Get a quick overview of your day with everything in one message.

  • /briefing — today's calendar + tasks + overdue + energy budget
  • /today — smart triage: what needs attention right now
Tips & Tricks
  • Pull to refresh — pull down on any page to reload
  • Home screen: Add this page to your home screen for an app-like experience
  • Telegram /help — full command reference with interactive menu
  • Desktop view: Scroll to the bottom and tap "Switch to desktop view"
  • /capture tasks — brain-dump mode: rapid-fire task entry
  • /spoons — track your energy budget throughout the day
